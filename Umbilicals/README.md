
# Umbilicals for Fixed Gantry StealthChanger (Trident)

## Printing

### TPU

- Clip x2 Minimum (More clips or cable ties can be used to hold the cable and PTFE together over the length of the umbilical.)
- Terminator x1
- Terminator (Mirrored) x1

### ABS/ASA

- Face Plate x1
- Outer Plate x1
- Cover Left x1
- Cover Right x1
- LED Diffuser x1 (Optional)


## BOM

- M3x6 BHCS x2
- M3x10 SHCS x4
- M5x10 BHCS x2
- M3 Heat Insert x8
- M5 Roll-in T Nut x2
- 3x0.3mm Flat Spring Steel x2


## Instructions

### Step 1

Install 6 M3 heat inserts in to the Face Plate.

![step1](images/step1.png)

### Step 2

Install a heat insert in to each of the covers.

![step2](images/step2.png)

### Step 3 (Optional)

Install the diffuser in to the Face Plate.

![step3](images/step3.png)

### Step 4 (Optional)

Apply 1mm foam around the perimiter of the Face Plate.

![step4](images/step4.png)

### Step 5

Bend a piece of 3x0.3mm spring steel 90 degrees and feed it through the faceplate. To get the correct length, the spring steel should reach the inside of the printers frame when the faceplate is in place.

![step5](images/step5.png)

### Step 6

Secure both pieces of spring steel with a m3x6mm BHCS screw.

![step6](images/step6.png)

### Step 7

Install a TPU Clip and Terminatior ensuring the orientation is correct. The cable should be on the bottom and PTFE on top.

![step7](images/step7.png)

### Step 8

Put a small bend in the end of the spring steel to keep the TPU Terminator in place.

![step8](images/step8.png)

### Step 9

Attach the Outer Plate to the printer using 2 M5x10 BHCS screws and 2 M5 roll-in T nuts.

![step9](images/step9.png)

### Step 10 ( Optional)

Install a SC Barf LED.

![step10](images/step10.png)

### Step 11

Attach the Face Plate to the Outer Plate using 4 M3x10 BHCS screws.

![step11](images/step11.png)

### Step 12

Feed the cables and PTFE tubes through and attach them to the toolheads. Once an umbilical length is determined place the cables and PTFE tubes in to their respective TPU Clip and Terminatior.

### Step 13

Slot the Covers in place and secure with a M3x8 BHCS screw.

![step13](images/step13.png)

### Step 14

Secure umbilical at the rear of the printer with a ziptie.

![step14](images/step14.png)
